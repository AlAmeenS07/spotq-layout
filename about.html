The SpotQ Story

We're on a mission to transform the way restaurants and customers experience dining queues.

We've all been there.

You arrive hungry and excited at a high-traffic restaurant, only to be met with a frustratingly long waitlist. You crowd around the entrance, straining to hear your name being called, unable to leave or relax.

That is exactly why we built SpotQ. We believe the dining experience should start the moment you decide where to eat — not just when you sit down.

By allowing customers to join the waitlist remotely and track their live status, we eliminate the physical crowd and the anxiety of waiting. Experience dining made elegant.
