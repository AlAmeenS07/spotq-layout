The SpotQ Story

We're on a mission to help restaurants master their front-of-house operations and elevate the guest experience.

The chaos ends here.

Your hosts are overwhelmed, the lobby is packed with anxious guests, and walk-aways are hurting your bottom line. Traditional waitlists and pagers just don't cut it anymore.

That is exactly why we built SpotQ. We believe managing your crowd shouldn't be chaotic.

By empowering guests to join your waitlist via QR and track their live status, we eliminate the physical crowd and host anxiety. Experience restaurant ops made elegant.
